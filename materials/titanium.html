Titanium Alloy Specifications

Each titanium grade offers unique performance characteristics for extreme aerospace and medical applications

Ti-6Al-4V (Grade 5)

Aerospace

The workhorse of the titanium industry with an exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and excellent fatigue resistance. The gold standard for aerospace structures and high-performance applications.

Tensile Strength 130 ksi
Yield Strength 120 ksi
Density 0.16 lb/in³
Machinability Good

Key Applications

  • Aircraft structural components
  • Aerospace fasteners
  • Gas turbine engines
  • Medical implants

CP Grade 2 Titanium

Biocompatible

Commercially pure titanium with excellent biocompatibility and corrosion resistance. The preferred choice for medical implants and chemical processing equipment.

Tensile Strength 50 ksi
Yield Strength 40 ksi
Density 0.163 lb/in³
Machinability Excellent

Key Applications

  • Medical and dental implants
  • Surgical instruments
  • Chemical processing equipment
  • Marine components

Ti-6Al-2Sn-4Zr-2Mo

High Temp

High-temperature titanium alloy with exceptional creep resistance and thermal stability. Designed for extreme aerospace applications where temperature resistance is critical.

Tensile Strength 150 ksi
Yield Strength 140 ksi
Density 0.165 lb/in³
Max Temperature 800°F

Key Applications

  • Jet engine components
  • High-temperature structures
  • Compressor discs
  • Gas turbine blades
