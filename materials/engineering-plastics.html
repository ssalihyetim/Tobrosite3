Engineering Plastic Material Specifications

Each engineering plastic offers unique chemical resistance and performance characteristics for specialized applications

PTFE (Teflon®)

Non-Stick

Polytetrafluoroethylene with exceptional chemical inertness and non-stick properties. The ultimate choice for chemical processing, food contact, and low-friction applications.

Max Temperature 500°F
Density 0.077 lb/in³
Chemical Resistance Excellent
Machinability Good

Key Applications

  • Chemical processing equipment
  • Food contact surfaces
  • Electrical insulators
  • Low-friction bearings
  • Gaskets and seals

Machining Characteristics

  • Requires sharp cutting tools
  • Low coefficient of friction
  • Chemical inertness to all solvents
  • FDA compliant grades available

PEEK

High Performance

A high-performance thermoplastic with exceptional mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and thermal stability. Ideal for extreme environments in aerospace and medical.

Tensile Strength 14 ksi
Density 0.047 lb/in³
Max Temperature 480°F
Machinability Good

Key Applications

  • Aerospace components
  • Medical implants
  • Semiconductor parts
  • High-performance bearings

POM (Delrin®)

Precision

Polyoxymethylene with excellent dimensional stability and mechanical properties. The ideal choice for precision gears, bearings, and mechanical components requiring tight tolerances.

Tensile Strength 10 ksi
Max Temperature 180°F
Density 0.051 lb/in³
Machinability Excellent

Key Applications

  • Precision gears
  • Mechanical fasteners
  • Valve components
  • Automotive parts
  • Consumer appliances

Machining Characteristics

  • Excellent dimensional stability
  • Superior surface finish
  • Low moisture absorption
  • Good fatigue resistance

UHMW-PE

Wear Resistant

Ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene with exceptional wear resistance and impact strength. Perfect for conveyor components, chute liners, and wear parts.

Impact Strength Very High
Abrasion Resistance Excellent
Density 0.034 lb/in³
Machinability Good

Key Applications

  • Conveyor components
  • Chute liners
  • Marine fenders
  • Cutting boards
  • Wear strips

Machining Characteristics

  • Exceptional impact resistance
  • Self-lubricating properties
  • Chemical resistance
  • Food grade available

Nylon

Versatile

A versatile engineering thermoplastic with good toughness, wear resistance, and chemical resistance. Used in a wide range of applications from automotive to consumer goods.

Tensile Strength 12 ksi
Density 0.041 lb/in³
Max Temperature 200°F
Machinability Good

Key Applications

  • Bearings and wear pads
  • Automotive engine covers
  • Textiles and fibers
  • Gears and rollers

Polycarbonate

Transparent

High-performance thermoplastic with exceptional clarity and impact resistance. Perfect for optical components, protective glazing, and transparent housings.

Impact Strength 18 ft-lb/in
Max Temperature 280°F
Clarity Excellent
Machinability Good

Key Applications

  • Optical lenses
  • Safety glazing
  • Electronic housings
  • Medical devices
  • Automotive lighting

Machining Characteristics

  • Excellent optical clarity
  • Superior impact resistance
  • Good dimensional stability
  • Flame retardant grades available
