Process Advantages

Why Choose CNC Turning?

01

High Precision

Achieve Swiss-style accuracy with tolerances as tight as ±0.00005" for critical medical and aerospace applications.

02

Excellent Surface Finish

Superior surface finishes with Ra values down to 8µin (0.2µm) straight off the machine, reducing secondary operations.

03

High Volume Production

Automated bar feeding and lights-out operation enable high-volume production with consistent quality and reduced labor costs.

04

Live Tooling Capability

Complete parts in one setup with live tooling for drilling, milling, and cross-hole operations, reducing handling and improving accuracy.

05

Material Efficiency

Optimized bar stock utilization and near-net-shape production minimize material waste and reduce overall part costs.

06

Rapid Turnaround

Quick setup changes and automated operation enable fast turnaround times for both prototypes and production runs.
